Sloppy Joes

A hamburger in which the minced-beef filling is made into a kind of meat sauce, typically with tomatoes and spices.

Serves
3
Cuisine
American
Time
20 mins
Author
Obama
Ingredients
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 6 sandwich buns
  • 2 tablespoons flour, or more
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
Steps
  1. Brown the beef in a skillet over medium heat; pour off fat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the brown sugar, vinegar, and mustard.
  3. Add this mixture to the beef along with the celery and onion, and ketchup. Stir together to combine.
  4. Sprinkle flour over mixture and stir to mix well. If mixture is still not thick enough to your liking, add a bit more flour until it is. Serve on buns.
